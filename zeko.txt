Zeko is a high-performance L2 ecosystem for zkApps, designed to enhance scalability and efficiency while maintaining security. 

Zeko leverages zero-knowledge proofs to enable secure and private transactions, supporting a wide range of applications from decentralized finance to gaming. 

Key features include:
- Fast transaction finality
- Low transaction fees
- Robust security through zk-SNARKs
